What is the Embed Widget?
The embed widget is a floating chat bubble that appears on your client's website.
After you build their bot, you give them one line of code to paste on their site.
Their customers then see a "💬 Ask Us" button — they click it, a chat window opens, and Infolio answers from the business's documents automatically.
① You upload client's PDFs / website
→
② You copy the embed code
→
③ Client pastes it on their site
→
✓ Bot is live!
